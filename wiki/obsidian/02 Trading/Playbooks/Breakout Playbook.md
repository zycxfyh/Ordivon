# Breakout Playbook

## Setup

- Price compresses below resistance or above support
- Narrative and structure align
- Liquidity is available for expansion

## Confirmation

- Volume expansion
- Strong close through level
- No immediate rejection

## Failure Signs

- Fast reclaim back inside range
- Open interest spike without follow-through
- One-sided crowding
